# How MUSE works

**MUSE** (Modular Unreal Studio Engine) lets you build inside **Helix Studio** together with an AI assistant.

You connect your assistant — like Claude or Cursor — to the Helix editor, then simply tell it what you want in plain language. It can look at your project, place and edit objects, set up your level, read the Helix documentation, and much more — right in the editor, while you watch.

_Made by Prysma Studio._

## The idea

MUSE adds a small connection point inside the Helix editor. Your AI assistant connects to it. When you ask the assistant to do something, your request goes to MUSE, MUSE carries it out in the editor, and the result comes straight back to you.

```
You  →  AI assistant  →  MUSE (inside Helix)  →  your project
```

You stay in control the whole time. The editor is open in front of you, so you can see every change as it happens.

## What you can ask for

- "Add a point light above the player start."
- "List everything in this level."
- "Move the car 200 units to the right."
- "How do I create a custom vehicle in Helix?" — it reads the docs for you.

…and a lot more. See [Example Prompts](example-prompts.md) for the full picture.
